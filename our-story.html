Our Story

Nestled in the heart of Trincomalee, Target Range Villa began as a family dream to create a sanctuary where guests could experience the warmth of Sri Lankan hospitality. Our journey started with a vision to blend modern luxury with the timeless beauty of the coast. Over the years, we have lovingly renovated and curated every corner of the villa, ensuring each guest feels at home.

From the lush gardens to the tranquil poolside, every detail tells a story of passion, dedication, and a desire to share the magic of our home with you. Whether you’re seeking relaxation, adventure, or a place to celebrate life’s special moments, Target Range Villa is your perfect escape.
